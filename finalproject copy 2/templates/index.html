Welcome to my basic Monopoly bot!


The goal of this bot is to develop a heat map of the Monopoly board.

Assume:
When the bot goes to jail, he will roll 3 times and then get out if no doubles are rolled.
There are equal chances of pulling any Chance or Community Chest card, even if it was just drawn.

To use, simply enter the amount of turns you would like the simulator to take.
